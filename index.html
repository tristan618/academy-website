About Academy Sports Club

Academy Sports Club is a youth-focused football program dedicated to excellence in football development, personal integrity, leadership, academic success, and community awareness. Our motto — “The Books and the Ball — You’ve Got It All!” — reflects our belief that education and athletic development go hand in hand.
